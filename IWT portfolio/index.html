E-DISPATCH AND MONITORING SYSTEM

E-DISPATCH AND MONITORING SYSTEM is a web-based application to monitor the movement of files and receipts and assist in their easy tracking. Its features include generation of receipts and files, updating its status, opening of new files, tracking the movement of files, dispatching letters/files, recording their track, etc. The system when implemented shall improve efficiency and effectiveness of the existing system, consistency of file records, resource management, and quality of administration.
